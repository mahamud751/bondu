# SocialConnect — Complete App Roadmap (Better than BIGO)

**Vision:** A safer, fairer, Bangladesh-first social-live platform that **beats BIGO** on creator economics, trust, private paid connection, and live entertainment — not a clone.

**Product thesis**

| BIGO strength | SocialConnect advantage |
| --- | --- |
| Live entertainment at scale | Live **+** premium 1:1 paid calls/chat (already ahead) |
| Gift spam economy | Transparent splits, honest wallet ledger, local rails |
| Light safety | Strong T&S, appeals, KYC, malware scan, contact filters |
| Global generic | Bangladesh payments (bKash/Nagad/SSLCommerz), local culture |
| Feature bloat | Guided creator tools, fair discovery, anti-bot |

---

## North-star product pillars

1. **Live Super Room** — chat, multi-guest, PK, audio rooms, ranking (BIGO parity+)
2. **Private Premium** — paid voice/video/chat packages (already stronger than BIGO)
3. **Fair Creator Economy** — clear revenue, hold policies, fast local withdrawals
4. **Trust & Safety OS** — moderation, fraud, age, reports (ops console already MVP)
5. **Retention Loop** — tasks, fan clubs, levels, events (engagement engine)
6. **Delight** — beauty filters, effects, entrance, AI host coach (polish layer)

## Definition of “complete app”

The app is **complete** when:

1. A new user can discover live → chat → gift → follow → claim tasks → join fan club  
2. A creator can go multi-guest / PK → earn → withdraw via local rails  
3. A fan can book a **private paid call** from a stream  
4. Trust & Safety can moderate live and 1:1 at scale  
5. Launch blockers (Phase 0) are closed with real provider acceptance
